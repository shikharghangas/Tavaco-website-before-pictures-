About TAVACO

Built on trust. Driven by quality. Serving our communities since 2009.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, TAVACO began as a small, local operation with one goal: to provide reliable, high-quality services that customers can count on.

What started as a modest neighborhood service has grown into one of the most trusted names in the property maintenance industry, proudly maintaining commercial and industrial sites, residential high-rises, and multi-unit development properties across Durham Region, York Region, and the Greater Toronto Area.

For over 15 years, TAVACO has built its reputation on consistency, professionalism, and genuine care for the communities we serve.

Our Commitment

From landscaping and construction to snow removal and waste management, every TAVACO project receives the same level of care—no matter the size.

We focus on safety, efficiency, and long-term value, ensuring every client receives dependable service year-round.
